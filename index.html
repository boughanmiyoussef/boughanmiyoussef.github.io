2024 - 2026

Holberton School

Immersed in intensive project-based learning, specializing in AI & Machine Learning. Mastered full-stack development with MERN stack and deep learning with TensorFlow/Keras.

I'm a software engineer who has specialized in AI and Machine Learning, building intelligent systems and full-stack applications. My recent work includes developing ShopEase, a production-ready e-commerce platform using the MERN stack, and diving deep into machine learning with Python, TensorFlow, and Keras. I am most fluent in JavaScript (ES6+), Python, React, Node.js, and leveraging cloud services to create scalable solutions.

My path to engineering is unique; I initially studied finance at the university level before pivoting to pursue a more creative and constructive field. A key achievement was my decision to commit to Holberton School's intensive program, where I fully immersed myself in project-based learning. This shift demonstrated my ability to adapt and dedicate myself to mastering complex new disciplines from the ground up.

I am motivated by the process of turning abstract problems into elegant, functional software. I believe this focus on tangible results, combined with my collaborative mindset from peer programming, allows me to positively impact a team. I strive to contribute clean, well-documented code and a perspective geared towards practical implementation and continuous learning.
